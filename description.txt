[h1]Do you want to add a Flag to the mod?[/h1]

If you would like to see some more flag in the game, and you have a flag ready, you can send me your flags and I'm going to add these to the mod and I'm giving credit to you in the workshop description (this section).

Flag Creation
The Files needed for this mod are .png files or .dds files (make sure the background is transparent).

You need to include multiple sizes of your flag:
- 256 x 256 (map)
- 128 x 128 (default)
- 24 x 24 (small)

The Files need the following colors
- map: white
- default: gold
- small: gold

Send me the files
To message me, just pm me on steam, you can upload the files to e.g. a cloud-service (imgur.com)
